Get To Know Us

They started the band in 2002 and since then have visited almost all European country and several countries located on the other continents. They have released three albums. Their concerts attract a large audience.

Boogie Boys played as a support or back-up band with many famous blues artists such as: Shakin’ Stevens, Ray Manzarek (The Doors), Eric Burdon (The Animals), Chris Barber, Phill Guy, Studebaker John, James Harman, Rod Piazza, Big Bill Morgenfield, The Mofo Party Band, Mike Sanchez, Ana Popovic.

In February 2009 they were the only non-U.S. band who reached the finals of the biggest blues contest in the world “International Blues Challenge- Memphis, USA”. In 2011 they represented Poland on the “1st European Blues Challenge” in Berlin where they reached the TOP 3. In 2012 they were awarded the title of “The Band of the Year” in the ranking “Blues Top 2011”.

Their newest album “Made in Cali” was produced by John Clifton in the USA. In 2013 “Made in Cali” was considered to be “The Album of the Year” in the ranking “Blues Top 2012”.
